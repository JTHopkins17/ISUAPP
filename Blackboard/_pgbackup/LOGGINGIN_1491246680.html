Having Troubles Logging In?

Your Blackboard login information (Username and Password) is the same login information you use to login to your MyISU Portal account.

If you have forgotten this information then please contact the Technology Support Center at (812)237-2910.

1. Have you changed your password recently? The system requires you to change your password every 180 days to avoid security issues. If you believe that your password has expired, please follow this link, https://isuportal.indstate.edu/apps/selfService/resetPassword.jsp, to change your password and gain access back into your account.
